SymmetricZOI case for below ground competition concept.

Symmetric Zone Of Influence with trees occupying the same node of the grid share the below-ground resource of this node equally (BETTINA geometry of a tree assumed). See Peters 2017: ODD protocol of the model BETTINA IBM
