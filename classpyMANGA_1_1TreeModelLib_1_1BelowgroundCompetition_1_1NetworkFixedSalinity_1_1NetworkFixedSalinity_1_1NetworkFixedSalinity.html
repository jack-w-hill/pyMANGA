def pyMANGA.TreeModelLib.BelowgroundCompetition.NetworkFixedSalinity.NetworkFixedSalinity.NetworkFixedSalinity.calculateBelowgroundResources (   self)

This function returns a list of the growth modification factors of all trees.

Calculated in the subsequent timestep.
The factor is > 1, if trees receive water from their adjacent trees; < 1 if the lose water to the adjacent tree; or = 1 if no exchange happens

Returns
: np.array with $N_tree$ scalars
